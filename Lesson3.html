Dots, Dots, Dots and Dots ...

Artist YAYOI KUSAMA is obsessed by the dot, but she created art out of her obsession.

You can create a dot with the point function. 

      point(x, y);

The point function has two params:

  • the x-position; how far in pixels from the left border?
  • the y-position; how far in pixels from the top?

But before you can draw a dot you have to get your tools ready. You need a stroke function, so your drawingtool knows which color to use. You need a strokeWeight function, to tell your drawingtool how big your dot should be.

Remember: position (0,0) is in the top-left-corner. 

  function setup(){
    createCanvas(300,300);
  }

  function draw(){
    stroke(100);
    strokeWeight(130);
    point(75, 100);

    stroke(100);
    strokeWeight(300);
    point(100, 300);

    stroke(100);
    strokeWeight(130);
    point(225, 100);

    stroke(100);
    strokeWeight(300);
    point(200, 300);

  //three dots

    stroke(0);
    strokeWeight(20);
    point(100, 100);

    stroke(100);
    strokeWeight(20);
    point(150, 100);

    stroke(200);
    strokeWeight(20);
    point(200, 100);
  }
